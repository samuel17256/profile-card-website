Biography

Dev Nanbam grew up with a deep curiosity about how things work — a trait that naturally led her into software engineering. She pursued a BSc in Computer Science from Bayero University Kano, graduating with first-class honours in 2019.

Since then she has worked across startups and agencies, honing her craft across the full web development stack. She is particularly passionate about developer experience — writing clean, maintainable code and building tools that make teammates more productive.

Outside of work, she mentors junior developers in a local Kano tech community, contributes to open-source projects on GitHub, and writes about software architecture on her blog.
